Respond to situation

Respond politely and clearly to the situation. Include:

1) A short introduction saying why you are contacting the person.
2) The key details of the problem, request, or situation.
3) A clear action you want (solution, advice, permission, information, next steps).
4) A polite closing showing appreciation or willingness to cooperate.

How to Practice with this Page

Press “Start”. The prompt will play once. Ten seconds after the audio finishes, you will hear a beep. Then you have 40 seconds to respond.

Use speech-to-text to see what you said and self-correct. It clears when you press “Try again” or “Next” (copy it out first if you want to keep it).

Listening settings

To keep the listening activities in the correct native accent, change your device language and voice settings to English (Australia/UK) and turn off any personalised or adaptive speech features. This stops your phone or laptop from switching to your own accent and ensures all audio and dictation stay in the native model your teacher intends.

If speech-to-text accuracy drops, double-check your browser microphone permissions and the selected input device.
