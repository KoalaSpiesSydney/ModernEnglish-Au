Respond to situation

Respond politely and clearly to the situation. Include:

1) A short introduction saying why you are contacting the person.
2) The key details of the problem, request, or situation.
3) A clear action you want (solution, advice, permission, next steps).
4) A polite closing showing appreciation or willingness to cooperate.

How to practise

Press Start. The audio prompt plays once automatically. After it finishes, you have 10 seconds to get ready — then a beep signals your 40-second response window. Use speech-to-text or type your answer. Press Repeat prompt to redo the same situation, or Next to move on.
