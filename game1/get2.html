Expressions with Get – Matching Game

Flip two cards to match each expression with its meaning. Each round uses 9 cards selected from the full set.

Teacher Notes / Full Expression List
  • get the impression (that…) – believe something based on clues
  • get the chance to… – have an opportunity
  • get the hang of something – learn how to do something well
  • get permission to… – receive approval
  • get the point – understand the main idea
  • get the joke – understand the humour
  • get the most out of something – use something effectively
  • get something done – arrange for something to be completed
  • get someone’s attention – make someone notice
  • get something straight – clarify information
  • get ahead – make progress, succeed
  • get around to (doing) – finally do something you delayed
  • get away with (something) – avoid punishment
  • get through to (someone) – make someone understand
  • get over with (something) – finish something unpleasant
  • get by on (something) – survive with limited resources
  • get into (something) – become interested or involved
  • get out of (doing something) – avoid doing something
  • get back at (someone) – take revenge
  • get across (an idea) – communicate clearly

Gap Fill Practice

Complete each sentence with the correct get phrase.

1) Have you ever committed a crime? Did you ______?

Answer: get away with it

2) Have you ever had your heart broken? Did you ______?

Answer: get over it

3) How much money do you ______ each day?

Answer: get by on
